Game Overview

Ship Shooter is an engaging arcade shooter where you pilot a single ship battling through endless waves of enemies and powerful bosses. Using simple controls (arrow keys + space to shoot), you dodge enemy bullets and collect powerups to enhance your firepower.

The game combines thrilling action, strategic powerup use, and intense boss battles for hours of entertainment.

How to Play

  1. Use the Arrow Left and Arrow Right keys or A and D keys to move your ship.
  2. Press the Spacebar to fire bullets at enemies.
  3. Destroy waves of enemies and powerups will occasionally drop ‚Äî collect them for advantages.
  4. Use powerups like Shield (temporary invincibility), Rapid Fire (faster shooting), and Double Bullet (two shots).
  5. Beat boss enemies every few waves to advance.
  6. Try to survive as long as possible and rack up a high score!

Key Features

Responsive Controls

Fluid and responsive movement with keyboard and precise shooting controls.

Dynamic Enemy Waves

Enemies spawn in strategic, challenging waves, escalating as you progress.

Epic Boss Fights

Bigger, fierce bosses with multiple attack patterns and phase changes.

Powerups & Bonuses

Collect powerups to boost fire rate, double bullets, or gain a shield.

Easy Mode

Balanced difficulty for casual gamers with smooth gameplay and forgiving lives.
